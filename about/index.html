About

I am a post-doctoral researcher within the Chair of Mechanical Process Engineering at the Otto-von-Guericke-Universitat Magdeburg. My research focusses on the numerical modelling of multi-phase flows, with contributions ranging from the modelling of surface-tension dominated interfacial flows to the modelling of particulate flows using immersed-boundary methods and/or Lagrangian particle-tracking. I also teach courses on process engineering and the simulation of mechanical engineering processes.
